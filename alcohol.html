Foster and Kinship Care Education

Offered through Hartnell Community College

411 Central Avenue; Salinas, CA 93901

Tel (831) 755-6709; To register, call (831) 755-6807

Website: www.harnell.edu

Services:

Mentor Moms and Dads are mentors who work with parents who have substance abuse problems and have lost custody of their children through the courts, but now have the opportunity for reunification. mentor moms and dads offer emotional support, advice, and guidance to the parents as they work their way through the legal process. Foster and adoptive mentors are experienced foster and adoptive parents who provide support for families who are fostering or adopting at-risk child.
